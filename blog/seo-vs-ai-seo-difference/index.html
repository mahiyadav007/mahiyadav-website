SEO vs AI SEO: What's the Difference and Which One Do You Need?

By Mahi Yadav·May 28, 2026·8 min read

"Do I need SEO or AI SEO?" It's the question I hear most often in 2026 — and the honest answer is that the two aren't rivals. They're layers of the same strategy. But they do optimise for different outcomes, and understanding the difference helps you invest your budget where it counts.

Let's break down what each one is, how they differ, and how to decide what your business should prioritise.

What is Traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO is the practice of improving your website so it ranks higher in search engine results pages. It covers technical health (speed, crawlability, indexing), on-page optimisation (keywords, content, metadata) and off-page authority (links and mentions). The goal is simple: appear higher in the list of results and earn the click. Learn more on my SEO services page.

What is AI SEO?

AI SEO is the practice of optimising your content to appear inside AI-generated answers — Google AI Mode, AI Overviews and assistants like ChatGPT and Gemini. Rather than competing for a link position, you're working to be the trusted source the AI summarises and cites. See my AI SEO services for the full approach.

How They Work Together

Here's the key insight: AI SEO is built on the foundation of traditional SEO. AI engines draw on the same signals search has always valued — relevance, quality, structure and authority. You can't shortcut to AI citations without solid SEO underneath. Likewise, ignoring AI SEO means watching visibility erode as more searches end inside an AI answer.

Traditional SEO earns the click. AI SEO earns the recommendation. You want both.

Which One Does Your Business Need?

  • If your SEO foundation is weak: start with traditional SEO — fix the technical issues, build quality content and authority first.
  • If your SEO is solid but AI visibility is low: layer AI SEO on top to capture citations and stay present in AI answers.
  • If you're starting fresh: build both together from day one — it's far more efficient than retrofitting later.

For most businesses in 2026, the right answer is "both, in the right order." A quick audit makes the priority obvious.

The Bottom Line

SEO and AI SEO aren't an either/or decision. Traditional SEO keeps you visible and clickable in search; AI SEO keeps you cited and recommended in AI answers. Together they future-proof your visibility as search keeps evolving — and the brands that invest in both now will be hardest to displace later.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AI SEO better than traditional SEO?
Neither is better — they work together. Traditional SEO wins rankings and clicks; AI SEO wins citations and visibility in AI answers. Most businesses need both, built on the same strong foundation.
Do I need AI SEO if my traditional SEO works well?
Yes, increasingly. As more searches are answered by AI, ranking alone no longer guarantees visibility. AI SEO ensures you stay present where users are shifting their attention.
Can I do AI SEO without traditional SEO?
Not effectively. AI engines rely on the same quality, structure and authority signals as search. Solid traditional SEO is the foundation AI SEO builds on.
