What is AI SEO?

AI SEO is the practice of optimising your website so it appears inside AI-generated answers — Google AI Mode, AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity — not just the traditional list of ten blue links.

When someone asks a question today, AI increasingly answers it directly and cites a handful of trusted sources. AI SEO makes sure your brand is one of them — structuring your content, entities and authority so the model understands you, trusts you and recommends you.

FAQ

AI SEO Questions, Answered

What is AI SEO?
AI SEO optimises your website and content so it ranks inside AI-powered search experiences like Google AI Mode and AI Overviews — not just the traditional blue links. The goal is to be the trusted source AI summarises and cites.
How is AI SEO different from traditional SEO?
Traditional SEO ranks a page in a list. AI SEO makes your content the answer the AI generates — which means structured content, clear entities, strong E-E-A-T and citation-worthy information rather than keywords alone.
Will AI search replace traditional SEO?
It changes SEO rather than replacing it. Search isn't disappearing — it's being answered by AI. Brands that adapt now will own visibility as zero-click search grows.
Does AI SEO work for small businesses?
Yes. AI engines reward clarity, expertise and trust — not just big budgets. A focused local business with well-structured, authoritative content can absolutely get cited in AI answers for its niche.
How do you get a brand cited by AI?
By structuring content to directly answer questions, strengthening entity and schema signals, building topical authority, and earning the trust signals AI models rely on when choosing which sources to quote.
