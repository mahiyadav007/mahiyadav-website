FAQ

Meta Ads Questions, Answered

How much should I spend on Meta Ads?
Enough to exit the learning phase and gather conversion data — then scale what works. After a free audit I'll recommend a realistic starting budget for your goals and market.
Facebook or Instagram — which is better for my business?
Both run through Meta and the best mix depends on your audience and creative. I test placements across both and let performance data decide where your budget works hardest.
Do Meta Ads work for lead generation?
Yes — extremely well when the funnel and creative are right. I build lead campaigns with strong targeting, compelling creative and proper tracking to keep cost per lead low and quality high.
What creative do you need from me?
I guide the creative strategy and can work with your existing assets or a brief. Strong Meta creative — especially Reels and short video — is the biggest lever on performance, so we invest there.
How do you track results accurately?
I set up the Meta Pixel and Conversions API (CAPI) for reliable, privacy-resilient tracking, so attribution stays accurate even with browser and iOS restrictions.
Do you offer a free Meta Ads audit?
Yes. I'll review your account or plan, spot wasted spend and creative opportunities, and show you the path to profitable Meta campaigns — free of charge.
