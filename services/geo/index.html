What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization is the practice of getting your brand cited and recommended inside generative AI engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini and Google AI Mode.

Where SEO targets a ranking in a list of links, GEO targets being the source the AI quotes when it composes an answer. That means optimising for entity clarity, citable facts, structured data and the authority signals language models rely on to decide who to trust.
