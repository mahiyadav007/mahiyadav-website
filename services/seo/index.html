What is SEO — and Why It Still Wins

Search Engine Optimization is the practice of earning visibility in Google's organic results — the listings you don't pay for. Done right, it turns search demand that already exists into a steady stream of qualified traffic, leads and sales.

Unlike ads that stop the moment you stop paying, SEO builds an asset. Every optimised page, every earned link and every piece of authority compounds — so your cost per acquisition keeps falling as your rankings keep climbing. In the AI search era, that organic authority is also what gets your brand cited inside Google AI Mode and AI Overviews.
