FAQ

Google Ads Questions, Answered

How much should I budget for Google Ads?
It depends on your industry, competition and goals. I recommend starting with a budget that gathers meaningful conversion data, then scaling what works. After a free audit I'll suggest a realistic starting budget and expected returns.
What is a good ROAS for Google Ads?
It varies by margin and industry, but most of my e-commerce clients target 4× or higher return on ad spend. For lead generation I optimise toward a target cost per qualified lead instead.
How is your management fee structured?
Transparent and simple — a flat monthly fee or a percentage of ad spend, agreed upfront. No hidden markups on your media budget. You always own your ad account and data.
How quickly will I see results?
Google Ads can drive traffic from day one. Profitable, optimised performance usually takes 2–4 weeks as campaigns gather conversion data and bidding stabilises.
Which campaign types do you manage?
Search, Display, Shopping/Performance Max, YouTube and remarketing — the full Google Ads ecosystem, chosen based on where your customers are and your funnel stage.
Do you offer a free Google Ads audit?
Yes. I'll review your existing account or plan a new one, identify wasted spend and quick wins, and show you the path to a profitable ROAS — free of charge.
